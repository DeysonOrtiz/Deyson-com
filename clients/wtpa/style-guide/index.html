Our Mission

We believe stories shape the soul of our culture, not just for today, but for generations to come. At Worth the Price of Admission, we set out to discover and preserve the voices of those shaping the world: the dreamers, the doers, the resilient, and the real.

Through raw, honest, and conversational documentaries, we dive beneath the surface to uncover the journeys that define lives. The setbacks. The breakthroughs. The quiet moments that carry the most significant weight.

We are not chasing headlines; we are chasing truth. We seek the story behind the spotlight. The kind of wisdom that does not expire.

Because one day, these stories will be the artifacts future generations look back on, not just to remember, but to learn, grow, and be inspired.

This is more than media. This is legacy work. Welcome to Worth the Price of Admission, where every story is a gift to the future, and every moment is worth preserving.

About Jill Martin

Jill was a photographer for Live Nation, and she also lived in an environment surrounded by prominent and fascinating individuals. This unique setting not only influenced her artistic perspective but also allowed her to forge meaningful connections and gain invaluable experiences throughout her career.

Jill has always been fascinated by the stories of others and the paths those individuals have taken to reach their current positions. This show focuses on the friends she has made, the celebrities she has encountered, and the extraordinary individuals who have crossed her path.

Inspired and motivated by her husband, Paul, she is here to take you behind the scenes, to the heart of what truly makes each tale "Worth the Price of Admission."

Typography

Elegant Headlines, Clean Body Text

Corda Light
Primary headline font
Elegant, sophisticated, warm

Corda Light brings a contemporary elegance to our brand communications. Its refined curves and clean lines ensure our headlines are both readable and distinctive.

Helvetica Regular
Body and supporting text
Clean, readable, timeless

Helvetica Regular offers clarity and versatility. Known for its modern lines, it ensures our communications are easily readable and professional across all platforms.

Typography Hierarchy

  • H1: Corda Light, 44 pixels, Rose for main headlines and page titles
  • H2: Corda Light, 32 pixels, Rose for section headers
  • H3: Corda Regular, 24 pixels, Ink for subsection headers
  • Body: Helvetica Regular, 16 pixels, Ink for all body copy
  • Captions: Helvetica Regular, 14 pixels, Ink for image captions and small text

Voice and Tone

Raw, Honest, and Conversational Communication

Voice Characteristics

  • Raw and Honest: We embrace authenticity over polish. Our conversations dig beneath the surface to reveal real, unfiltered human experiences.
  • Conversational and Intimate: We speak as if sharing stories over coffee; warm, engaging, and personal.
  • Respectful and Honoring: Every story is treated with dignity. We recognize the courage it takes to share personal journeys.
  • Truth-Seeking: We are not chasing headlines; we are chasing truth. We seek the story behind the spotlight.
  • Legacy-Minded: We create content that stands the test of time; wisdom that does not expire.
"We believe stories shape the soul of our culture, not just for today, but for generations to come."

Communication Guidelines

  • Dive beneath the surface: Ask questions that reveal the setbacks, breakthroughs, and quiet moments that carry weight
  • Be conversational: Write and speak naturally, as if having an intimate conversation
  • Focus on the journey: Highlight the path taken, not just the destination
  • Keep it real: Embrace imperfection and vulnerability over manufactured perfection
  • Think generationally: Create content that future generations will look back on to learn, grow, and be inspired
