Look and Sound Your Best on Camera.

A four-week, small group workshop for professionals seeking to refine their lighting, sound, and delivery so their natural personality shines through clearly.

Workshop Introduction

What Is Included

  • Four weekly live group sessions
  • Personalized setup and lighting review
  • Coaching on natural delivery and authentic presence
  • Assignments to apply each lesson
  • Final feedback and a recording of your improved on camera performance

What You Will Learn

Look professional using the gear you already own

You do not need expensive equipment to create high quality videos. Learn how to optimize your current setup with simple adjustments to lighting, framing, and positioning.

Improve your sound so your message is always clear

Discover microphone techniques, room acoustics, and recording practices that ensure your voice sounds warm, clear, and professional in every video.

Bring out your natural personality and presence

Learn how to relax, breathe, and communicate authentically on camera so your true personality connects with your audience.

Create a setup that reflects your brand and style

Design a video environment that feels professional, personal, and aligned with who you are and what you represent.

Workshop Schedule

Week 1: Setup, Lighting, and Framing

Learn how to position your camera, optimize your lighting, and frame your shots to create a professional, polished look.

Week 2: Sound and Clarity

Discover how to capture clear, natural audio and eliminate distractions so your message comes through with warmth and precision.

Week 3: Delivery and Connection

Develop the confidence and presence that helps you communicate naturally and connect authentically with your viewers.

Week 4: Final Recording and Feedback

Put everything together in a final recording session where you will receive personalized feedback and see your progress.

Why Both Matter

Most workshops focus on either the technical side or the confidence side. This workshop brings both together because one without the other falls short.

Great lighting and sound mean nothing if your presence feels stiff or uncertain. And all the confidence in the world cannot overcome poor audio or distracting visuals.

When you master both the technical fundamentals and the emotional presence, something powerful happens. Your videos feel professional and authentic at the same time. Your audience can focus on your message because nothing technical distracts them, and they trust you because your presence feels genuine.

This is the complete approach. Technical skill creates credibility. Authentic presence creates connection. Together, they create trust.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this workshop a recurring program?

No, this is a focused four week workshop designed to deliver lasting results.

What if I already feel confident on camera?

This workshop helps you enhance your lighting, sound, and setup so your confidence translates beautifully on screen.

Do I need expensive equipment?

No. You can start with what you already have. I will show you how to achieve the best results from your current setup and suggest simple upgrades that can make a significant difference.

Will I get personal feedback?

Yes. During each live session, you can ask questions, share your setup, and receive direct guidance to help you look and sound your best.

What if I miss a session?

Recordings are available after every session, along with notes and key takeaways. You can review them at any time, so you never fall behind.

How is this different from online tutorials?

Most tutorials show you what to do. This workshop is interactive and personal, blending hands-on practice with feedback that helps you master both the tools you use and the presence that builds trust on camera.
