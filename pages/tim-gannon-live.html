The Defining Moment

The Million-Dollar Check

"I had to take courage deep down, as deep as you go, to find the courage to rip that check up."

Tim was drowning in debt. A quarter million dollars at eighteen percent interest. A four-year-old and two-year-old at home. His boss offered him a blank check, anything under a million dollars.

For twenty minutes, Tim stared at that check. It would eliminate his debt, secure his family's future, buy them a house. But it came with invisible chains.

"That's not the way I'm going to earn my money. I'm going to earn it with someone, not for someone."

The Choice

That torn check became the foundation of a four billion dollar empire. Sometimes your greatest moment of courage looks like your biggest mistake.

The Man Behind the Story

From Valet to Visionary

"I changed my name in third grade from John to Tim. That was the first thing I remember doing to sort of recreate who I was." From that early act of self-determination to building a global empire, Tim's story is about the courage to reinvent yourself.

Founder of Outback Steakhouse, five-time U.S. Open Polo Champion, and world number one player at age fifty. But the real story is not the achievements, it's the principles that made them inevitable.

"Dreams do not expire. They just wait for you to find the courage to pursue them."

The Philosophy

"When you don't have a father, mothers teach you right from wrong. But fathers teach you how to make money."

Tim learned to find mentors everywhere and turn every setback into a setup for something greater.
